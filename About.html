About OnlineShopDesigners

Since the beginning,OSD has been dedicated to helping entrepreneurs and businesses push the boundaries of commerce and thereby achieve their ambitions. With more than 300,000 ecommerce sites worldwide, OSD is the leading ecommerce platform in Europe and Latin America. In 2019, OSD sites generated more than 17 billion euros in online sales.

Our culture

We are passionate about the web, ecommerce, coding, open source, and are fully committed to both our community and our partners. Our head office is located in a large Parisian building built at the end of the 19th century, in which a market was originally based. These offices have been keeping commerce alive for more than 100 years: this link between past and future commerce has been part of the OSD philosophy since it was founded.

The team

Like our community, the OSD team is multicultural. This diversity is our strength. We share the same boldness, passion and open-mindedness and are constantly on the lookout for new talent and ideas. Our goals are ambitious and our team evolves quickly, so we are always looking for new talent.
